Love Islands Chloe Burrows is unrecognisable in childhood throwback snap shared by best friend

May 11, 2024
blog

LOVE Island star Chloe Burrows has been spotted looking totally different in a childhood throwback snap shared by her best friend.

The marketing executive has caused quite a stir in the villa this week – having snuck in a kiss with semi-pro footballer Toby Aromolaran.

But in the photo shared by friends – who are in charge of her social media while she is away – to her Instagram stories, she looks totally innocent.

Answering a tell-all Q&A about the Love Island contestant, her pals revealed that Chloe is the “eldest of four” siblings, with one sister and two brothers.

“She might kill me for this one,” the mystery pal wrote alongside the sweet throwback, showing Chloe and her siblings as young children.

In the snap, a brunette Chloe can be seen smiling towards the camera while clutching youngest brother Nick, now 15, in her arms.

Wearing a black t-shirt and baggy jeans, the star looked a world away from her long-legged blonde look she has sported since entering the villa.

The post also detailed the ages of her two middle siblings, Max, 24, and Bridget, 20, who she shares a close bond with while back at home.

Last night, Chloe found herself in hot water as Toby, 22, finally came clean he had shared a snog with the blonde behind partner Kaz Kamwi’s back.

Semi-pro footballer Toby had been coupled up with Kaz, 24, since the beginning of the show, but Chloe stirred the pot in the recent challenge.

Having planted one on Toby’s lips, the two went off to spend more time together, sharing another secret snog in front of the cameras.

After Toby confessed to Kaz last night, Chloe also pulled her aside to check the two were able to get along in an awkward confrontation.

“You could have kissed him on the hand, you could have kissed him on the cheek. She did not need to kiss him on the lips,” Kaz told the cameras. “Don’t make me out like I’m a mug.”

She went on to make her feelings clearer in the Beach Hut, saying: “I don’t like slimy girls. I don’t like sly girls. I don’t like snakey girls. She’s all three.”

Tonight’s re-coupling could see Chloe and Toby form a fresh alliance, while new boy Teddy enters the villa to cause further drama amongst the girls.

Love Island continues tonight with it’s best bits show at 9pm on ITV2.
