Robert Albert Diaco (born February 19th 1973) is an American football coach currently serving as an assistant defensive coach for Purdue University and is a former linebacker. He played in the NCAA football tournament in UConn University of Iowa for coach Hayden Fry from 1992 to 1995. He was then Head Coach of UConn Huskies from 2014 to 2016. In 2015, Diaco’s Huskies beat undefeated Houston 20–17 at Rentschler Field to make the Huskies bowl-eligible for the first time since Edsall left. At this point many UConn fans believed that Diaco was the future of the program after taking a 2–10 team to a bowl game the next year.

Diaco was director of defense at Cincinnati during 2009, during the tenure of Brian Kelly. Cincinnati ended the year as 44th within NCAA Division I-A’s points per game allowed (23.1) as well as 23rd for defensive sacks (37.0).
