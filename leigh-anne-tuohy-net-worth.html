Leigh Anne Tuohy has a net worth of $25 million as of mid-2016. She was born Leigh Anne Roberts on August 9, 1960, in Memphis, Tennessee, USA. Besides being an interior designer and known for her appearances on the TV series “Extreme Makeover: Home Edition”, she is famous for being the legal guardian of NFL player Michael Oher.

Her wealth comes from her involvement in the entertainment industry and as the owner of a chain of fast-food restaurants with her husband. The couple owns over 80 fast-food restaurants, including popular franchises like Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, Long John Silver’s, and Kentucky Fried Chicken, mainly located in the southern USA.

Aside from her business ventures, Leigh Anne has also thrived as an interior designer, and her participation in the reality TV series “Extreme Makeover: Home Edition” from 2010 to 2012 significantly contributed to her net worth.

Leigh Anne Tuohy has maintained her personal life and has been married to former basketball player Sean Tuohy since 1982. The couple has two children and also adopted American Football player Michael Oher, who is now playing for the Carolina Panthers in the NFL. The whole family’s journey was chronicled in the book “The Blind Side: Evolution of a Game” and its subsequent film adaptation.

As for her education, Leigh Anne attended Briarcrest Christian School and the University of Mississippi, where she was a member of the Kappa Delta sorority and the cheerleading team.

In addition to her successful career and personal life, Leigh Anne has authored a book titled “In a Heartbeat: Sharing the Power of Cheerful Giving” with her husband Sean Tuohy and Sally Jenkins.
