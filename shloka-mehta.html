Career

Shloka Mehta completed her schooling at Dhirubhai Ambani International School, Mumbai. She attended Princeton University, New Jersey, United States, where she completed her bachelor’s degree in Anthropology. Later, she went to the London School of Economics and Political Science and received a Master’s degree in Law. After completing her education, she returned home and joined her father’s company Rosy Blue as one of the directors of the company in 2014. She also co-founded ConnectFor, a company dealing with the NGOs providing them with volunteers in terms of donation as well as manpower. As of 2018, she has an estimated net worth of $18 million or Rs. 120 crores.
