Biography/Wiki

The Capricorn model was born on 4 January 1991 (age 30 years; as of 2021) in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. [1] Since his childhood, he wanted to do something related to fashion.

At the age of 22, he moved from Noida to Mumbai to pursue a career in modelling.

Rohman Shawl doing ramp walk

Over the years, he has done ramp walks for many renowned fashion designers, including Sabyasachi Mukherjee.

He made his first acting appearance in Shilpa Shetty’s Amazon Prime Series Hear Me Love Me (2018).

Family, Caste & Girlfriend

Rohman was born into a middle-class Sunni-Muslim family with roots in Jammu & Kashmir. Not much is known about his family apart from the fact that his father’s name is Riyaz Shawl and his sister’s name is Nadia Shawl.

He first met Sushmita Sen at Neeta Lulla’s fashion show in Mumbai. Putting behind the age gap of around 15 years, it was Rohman who first approached her and after dating her for a couple of months, he proposed to Sushmita, which she accepted. The couple made their first public appearance together at Shilpa Shetty’s Diwali bash held in Mumbai.
