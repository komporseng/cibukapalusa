Little Mix Drops &quot;Woman Like Me&quot; Music Video With Nicki Minaj

July 01, 2024
blog

On October 26th at midnight, the ladies of Little Mix released their brand new music video for their first single off their brand new album LM5, “Woman Like Me” featuring Nicki Minaj and we are loving it.

“Woman Like Me” was directed by Marc Klasfeld, who has directed many music videos by A-List celebrities such as Katy Perry, Justin Timberlake, Jay-Z, and more.

The music video was shot at the Knebworth House country estate in Hertfordshire. The girls show off their dining etiquette at a dinner table filled with expensive silverware, amazing choreography, and iconic outfits. Perrie, Leigh-Anne, Jesy, and Jade never disappoint.

The estate where the music video was filmed gives off a Renaissance vibe and it is very aesthetically pleasing. Nicki Minaj makes an appearance in a painting while having two outfit changes singing along to her verse.

LM5 will be available to stream and purchase on November 16th. Check out the track list that the girls tweeted recently.
